Who are we?

We are a production club, meaning we teach everything under the alias of:

  • Video Editing
  • Graphic Design
  • Picture editing
  • and more!

We teach a skill that you won't find in a general class, or elective.
While you may think you know what goes on behind the scenes
of your favorite movie, you really don't, and we're here to
uncover their secrets.

With a brief presentation and live demo + practice time, you will be
able to identify any techniques, or recreate your favorite effect.

And even if you don't, at least you'll learn how to cut a video,
and turn it into a beautiful masterpiece to show friends!
